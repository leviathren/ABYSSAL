2023

2023 wasn't a very music-heavy year for me because I was so busy with life and changes going on. I finished school and walked in the summer and then the rest of the year I was spending time with my family and hanging out with them because for the last four years I've been out of state and would only see them over break, so I spent a lot of time catching up with them and watching shows with my dad and not listening to music for hours on end.

Favorite Songs:

I got heavily back into Pierce the Veil late in the year because I saw them live and first of all, best show I've been to hands-down, second of all, I got to see the live debut of Resilience, So Far So Fake, and The Jaws of Life!!!! And, according to setlist.fm, I saw Besitos performed live for the first time since 2013... O.o crazy how long ago that is now. I was tripping the whole time thinking about how I've been listening to them for over a decade now, because that makes it over half my lifetime.
