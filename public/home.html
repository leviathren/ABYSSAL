Make the descent...

You have surfed your way into the ABYSSAL zone of the web. This internet outpost is a collection of all things that interest me and a project dedicated to the Web Revival.

The Web Revival is all about making the web creative and personal again. Taking inspiration from Old Web aesthetics but building on progressive values, Web Revival spaces aim to move away from corporate control and towards a "spirit of opennes and fresh excitement", free from ads and hostile debate culture.

One of these spaces is the 32-Bit Cafe, a community of web enthusiasts working together to build a fun, friendly place on the internet dedicated to learning and sharing resources. Their Discourse Forum is now open!

Anyways, thanks for reading. Stay as long as you like.
