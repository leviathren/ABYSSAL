welcome, wanderer

hello weary traveler. this internet outpost is a passion project and collection of all things that interest me.

the making of this site itself is the main project, but you will also find some art i make here.

stay as long as you like.

the web revival movement is about reclaiming the personal internet and making spaces that welcome creativity and are free from big corporations and capitalist-driven content. one way to participate is by creating a personal site. personal sites give people the opportunity to decorate however they want and share whatever they want!

for a deeper understanding, these are great places to start:
the mass movement by yesterweb.org
intro to the web revival by melon king
