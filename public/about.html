about leviathren

hi i'm WREN, i go by leviathren online. you can also call me levi for short. i'm 22 years old and living in the Pacific Standard Time zone UTC-08:00.

some keystones of what makes me who i am is my ethnicity and queerness.

i am a second generation chinese american. my first language was cantonese, though i am hardly fluent now, heartbreakingly. this, with the added layer of growing up in a small, 80%-white town, was the first core piece in shaping me.

realizing that i loved women and wanted to share my life with another feminine soul (feminine as in sapphics, not exclusively femmes) was so liberating for me. it felt like i was finally meeting myself for the first time, the real, whole me. every bit of my person and what i do is through my queer, chinese background.

oh there's also the whole thing with gender, i won't get into it here (i'll probably write about all of this in blog posts) but basically what i tell people is that i'm genderfluid, and what i tell other queer people is: my gender? lesbian. my sexuality? lesbian. iykyk.
